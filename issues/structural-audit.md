## 1. Structural Contradictions & Overlaps

The biggest risk to a 24-month roadmap is redundant cognitive load. You have several tiles that say the exact same thing under different names, creating a false sense of variety.

**Block A (Freelancing) vs. Block B (Agency)**
*   **The Contradiction:** Block A has Convert to Retainer Model and Block B has Productize Offers. In reality, these are the exact same administrative task.
*   **The Fix:** Keep Block A strictly manual and hyper-individual. Change Block A's retainer box to "Secure 1 Reusable Tech Asset Pack" (saving your personal editing time). Keep Block B strictly operational and outsourced. Change Block B's outreach box to "Hire Inbound Landing Page Builder" to turn your traffic from active to passive.

**Block D (Screenplaying) vs. Block C (YouTube)**
*   **The Overlap:** Block C uses Filmmaker's Journal Series and Block D uses Adapt Personal Struggles to Fiction. While narratively beautiful, you are at risk of spending more time vlogging about writing than actually writing.
*   **The Fix:** Change Block C's box to "No-B-Roll Talking Head Mechanics" (forcing you to record fast, high-retention technical editing breakdowns with minimal production footprint). Keep Block D locked purely on cinematic scripts that require an actor, not a camera phone vlog.
